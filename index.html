Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, technologies, products, or processes that improve efficiency, solve problems, and meet societal needs.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs. It emphasizes environmental protection, economic resilience, and social equity.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the physical and organizational structures (e.g., transportation, energy, water systems, communication networks) necessary for a country’s operation and growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment focuses on enabling individuals and communities to take charge of their own development by providing access to resources, opportunities, and rights.

The Vision of Future

The future of a country built on digital transformation, green energy, and modern infrastructure is one of efficiency, sustainability, and inclusivity. This vision embodies a nation that leverages technology, harnesses renewable energy, and invests in advanced infrastructure to create a prosperous, resilient, and equitable society.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC) has recently announced new rules for satellite internet services, opening licenses to foreign firms. This move is designed to enhance internet connectivity across the country, particularly in remote and underserved areas, by leveraging satellite technology. The inclusion of foreign entities is anticipated to bring in expertise and investment, further boosting the nation's digital infrastructure.
